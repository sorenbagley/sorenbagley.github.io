Soren Bagley is a photographer based in Toledo, Ohio.

He co-founded the advertising agency Hawkesbury Media Group which produces commercial content for a variety of businesses in the Midwest. HMG has produced content for such high-profile clients as LifeTouch, Buckeye Cable Network, and the Republican National Committee.

Bagley is currently employed as an in-house photographer for Woodard Photographic where he works to capture a wide variety of candids for major events in his home city of Toledo, OH.
